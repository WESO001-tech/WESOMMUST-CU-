Our Doctrinal Basis

The fundamental truth of Christianity according to Titus 2:1

1. The Trinity

The unity of the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit in the God-head

Matthew 3:16-17; John 16:12-15

2. God's Sovereignty

The sovereignty of God in creation, revelation, redemption and final judgment

Daniel 4:25; Revelation 4:11; Colossians 1:15-19

3. Holy Scriptures

The divine inspiration and entire truthfulness of the Holy Scriptures

II Timothy 3:16-17

4. Universal Sinfulness

The universal sinfulness of all men since the fall

Romans 3:23-24

5. Redemption

Redemption from the guilt, penalty, dominion and pollution of sin solely through the Lord Jesus Christ

John 3:16; Romans 10:9

6. Resurrection

The bodily resurrection of the Lord Jesus Christ from the dead and His ascension

Acts 2:31-33
